Sample questions from Capital One with responses as the typical answer

1. What technical challenges might acquire ING Direct have to Capital One, related to our Technology / IT situation?
Response: Some challenges that come to mind are:
    * Both companies will have numerous systems, software, etc. that overlap such as billing, HR, 
      accounting, web sites, databases, etc. These will have to be simplified to reduce cost.
    * Both companies may use different internal systems like email benefits, reporting, etc. 
      that must be integrated and then consolidated. There will be training costs as well. 
    * Statements, cards, signs, ATMs, etc. will all need to be rebranded and updated.
    * The companies will have different data models and names for things.
